My Interests

I study how children develop curiosity, attention, early reasoning, and creative problem‑solving. I’m especially interested in how learning environments—and the technologies within them—shape cognitive habits.

Background

My work spans learning sciences, human–computer interaction, and curriculum design. I build tools, interfaces, and educational materials that support diverse learners, including neurodivergent students.
