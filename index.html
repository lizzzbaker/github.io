About Projects Writing

Hi, I’m Elizabeth.

I’m a designer and researcher interested in how children think, learn, and develop early cognitive strategies. My work blends learning sciences, human–computer interaction, curriculum design, and creative pedagogy.

I graduated from the University of Michigan — Ann Arbor with a B.S. in Computer Science and a Minor in Mathematics. I’ve taught and tutored students across a wide range of ages, and those experiences shaped my interest in how instructional design, cognitive load, curiosity, and neurodiversity intersect.

I love building learning tools, early-concept modules, and research-informed curriculum that help kids think more clearly, creatively, and confidently.
