Hi, I’m Elizabeth.

I design learning experiences, interfaces, and educational tools that help children think more clearly and creatively in a world shaped by technology.

My work blends learning sciences, human–computer interaction, and curriculum design. I care deeply about making educational tools feel intuitive, warm, and accessible — especially for neurodivergent learners.

This portfolio is where I explore ideas, share prototypes, and build small experiments as I prepare for doctoral study.
