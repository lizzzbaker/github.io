About Projects Writing

Hi, I’m Elizabeth.

I design learning experiences, interfaces, and educational tools that help children think more clearly and creatively in a world shaped by technology. I’m fascinated by how curiosity, attention, and early cognitive habits develop— and how beautifully designed systems can support a wide range of learners.

My work blends learning sciences, human–computer interaction, and curriculum design. I focus on making tools feel intuitive, warm, and accessible—especially for neurodivergent learners whose needs are often overlooked.

This portfolio documents the systems, research, and playful experiments I’m creating as I prepare for doctoral study.
