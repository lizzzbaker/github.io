About Projects Writing

Hi, I’m Elizabeth.

I design learning experiences, interfaces, and educational tools that help children think more clearly and creatively in a world shaped by technology.

I graduated from the University of Michigan–Ann Arbor with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Mathematics. Much of my work is grounded in curiosity, cognitive development, and human–computer interaction.

I love teaching and tutoring. I’ve supported learners across math, CS, and foundational problem-solving—and this hands-on experience deeply informs my design philosophy. I’m especially interested in research at the intersection of learning sciences and interaction design.

This site is where I explore ideas, share prototypes, and build small experiments as I prepare for doctoral study.
